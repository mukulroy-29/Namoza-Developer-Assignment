Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my first appointment?
Please bring any previous X-rays, MRI scans, or medical records related to your condition. Also, carry a valid government ID and your insurance card if applicable.
Do you accept health insurance?
Yes, we are empaneled with all major health insurance providers. Our dedicated desk will assist you with the claims process.
How long does a consultation usually take?
A standard initial consultation takes about 20 to 30 minutes, allowing our specialists to thoroughly understand your symptoms and medical history.
Are digital X-rays available on-site?
Yes, all our clinics are equipped with advanced digital X-ray technology to ensure quick and accurate on-the-spot diagnostics.
Do I need a referral to see a specialist here?
No, you do not need a doctor's referral to book a consultation at OrthoNow. You can book directly using our online form or by calling our clinics.
